What is CACFP?

A Federal Program Built Around Feeding Children Well

The Child and Adult Care Food Program (CACFP) is a federal nutrition program administered in Texas by the Texas Department of Agriculture. It was created to ensure that children in licensed care settings receive balanced, nutritious meals every single day — and it supports the centers that make that possible.

  • Children receive balanced, nutritious meals that meet USDA standards
  • Your center receives monthly reimbursements to sustain the program
  • Free to join — no cost ever to your center
  • We handle compliance monitoring and TDA paperwork for you

The Process

How It Works — In 4 Simple Steps

Getting started with CACFP through M&Ms is straightforward. We guide you through every step.

1

Apply Online

Fill out our simple contact form. We will reach out within one business day to review your center's eligibility and answer your questions.

2

Get Approved

We submit your application to TDA, obtain program approval, and handle all the enrollment paperwork. You just gather your center's documents.

3

Serve & Record

Serve CACFP-compliant meals and snacks daily. We train your team on meal patterns, menu planning, and attendance records that meet TDA requirements.

4

Receive Your Reimbursement

Submit your monthly meal counts through our process. We claim your CACFP reimbursements from TDA and disburse your monthly payment directly to your center — so you can reinvest in the children you serve.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Meals & More Support (M&Ms) is a CACFP food program sponsor located in Sugar Land, Texas. For privacy questions, contact us at mealsnmores@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • Information you provide: When you fill out our contact or interest form, we collect your name, email address, phone number, child care center name, city, and any other information you voluntarily provide.
  • Information collected automatically: We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized data about how visitors use this website, including pages visited, time on site, general geographic location (city/region level), device type, and referral source. Google Analytics does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name or email address.

3. Cookies and Google Analytics

This website uses cookies to enable Google Analytics. A cookie is a small text file placed on your device. Google Analytics uses first-party cookies (_ga, _gid, _gat) to distinguish users and track usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is transmitted through these cookies.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by: declining cookies in our cookie notice when you first visit the site; installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on; or adjusting your browser's cookie settings to block all cookies.

Google's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry about CACFP enrollment
  • To communicate program information relevant to your child care center
  • To improve this website using aggregated, anonymized analytics data

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with the Texas Department of Agriculture (TDA) as required to process your CACFP enrollment application, and with Google LLC for anonymized analytics data only.

6. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for the duration of our business relationship and up to 3 years afterward for program compliance purposes. Analytics data is retained in Google Analytics per Google's standard retention settings (up to 14 months by default).

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you by contacting us at mealsnmores@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable time.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Please do not send sensitive financial information through our contact form.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is directed at child care center operators and administrators, not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 13 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
